Micheal Jackson

Micheal's biography

Michael Jackson popularly known as the "King of Pop" was a multi-talented musical entertainer who enjoyed
a chart-topping career both with the Jackson 5 and as a solo artist. He released one of the best-selling albums in history, 'Thriller,' in 1982
and had other number-one hits on 'Bad' and 'Off the Wall'.

Notable Facts About Micheal

  • Occupation: Singer
  • Born on August 29, 1958 in Gary, Indiana.
  • Died on June 25, 2009 in Los Angeles, California.
  • Best known for Thriller, the best-selling album in history.
  • Nickname: King of Pop
  • Started his singing career at just eight years old.
  • His album thriller won ten grammy awards.
  • He was married twice with three children.
  • He purchased the rights to the Beetles catalogue in 1985 for $47 million.
  • Around 35 million of his albums were sold worldwide in the 12 months after he died.
